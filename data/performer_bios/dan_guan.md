**Dan Guan** is a San Francisco based comedian originally from Boston, Massachusetts. With his wit, he draws his humor from his own growing up experience as one of the few American-Born Chinese kids in his New England town with his immigrant family. Dan performs at venues locally and across the country, such as the Punch Line San Francisco, Cobb’s Comedy Club, and Don’t Tell Comedy. He has also appeared at the SF Sketchfest and the Riot Comedy Festival.
