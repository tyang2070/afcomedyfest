**Max & Nicky** are twin brothers Max & Nicky Weinbach, a quirky duo act with an absurd and offbeat sensibility. They’ve opened up for Sarah Silverman, Reggie Watts, Hannah Einbeinder, Sarah Sherman, Rachel Bloom, Moshe Kasher, Jo Firestone, Henry Phillips, Brent Weinbach, and DJ Douggpound and have appeared in the New York Comedy Festival’s *Comics to Watch*, the Netflix Is a Joke Festival, SF Sketchfest, the Very Big Very Asian Comedy Festival in New York, and the Vermont Comedy Festival. In addition to stand-up, Max & Nicky have written and produced two original full-length musicals, Max’s *A Match Made in Hell* and Nicky’s *Made in China.* Oh, and make sure to check out their solo, two-man show the next time they put it on. It’s called *Max & Nicky: A 60s Variety Show*, and it’ll really send you!
