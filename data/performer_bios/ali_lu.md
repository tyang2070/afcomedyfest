**Ali Lu** is a Filipina American stand‑up comedian, writer, and musician who ponders life’s big  
questions such as: Am I allowed to be naked in front of someone else’s dog? Is anyone else  
suffering from Adult Onset Poverty? And why is everyone’s anxiety cuter than mine?\!  
She was selected for the inaugural class of NBCU’s Thousand Miles Project (2022) and has  
written scripted rom‑com podcasts for FOX’s MeetCute since 2024\. As a stand‑up comic, she’s  
been featured on Netflix Is A Joke, Women Crush Wednesdays at The Hollywood Improv, the  
Sacramento Punchline, The Burbank Comedy Festival, and the Alaska B4UDie Festival (2019,  
2024).  
Ali marries music with comedy on the song‑parody game show Those Aren’t The Words,  
co‑hosts the comedy podcast Look It Up and performs tandem stand‑up with her partner Baldev  
Sandhu in their act Marital Sets (FKA Premarital Sets).  
As a classically trained vocalist, she sings all over Los Angeles independently and as a featured  
soloist with the Angeles Chorale under the direction of Lesley Leighton. Ali recently made her  
Carnegie Hall debut in March 2025 and performed alongside the LA Philharmonic in Home  
Alone Live at The Walt Disney Concert Hall in December 2025\.
