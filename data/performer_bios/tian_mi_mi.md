**Tian Mi Mi** is an all Mandarin speaking improv team that was initially formed to compete in a special Improv Cagematch event. After winning the competition, the members thought it would be fun to gather a group of mandarin speaking performers in the community to do shows together. It's both a way to allow the performers to experience doing improv in a different language, and also show that improv and comedy can still resonate even when the audience doesn't understand the language it's being performed in.
