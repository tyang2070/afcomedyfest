**Y’all We Asian** is Austin’s (and Texas’) first all Asian-American improv comedy troupe. Formed in 2017, Y'all We Asian sports a diverse cast of performers with family backgrounds hailing from all across Asia, the Pacific Islands, and the US. Y’all We Asian has been awarded The Austin Chronicle’s “Best of Austin’s Critics Pick for Best Rising Stars of Improv” as well as two B. Iden Payne awards for “Outstanding Resident Improvisational Troupe.” Y’all We Asian can be seen performing monthly at Fallout Theater in Austin, Texas and consists of Attiya Ali, Nick Fung, Sunny Huang, G-Su Paek, Ananya Roy, Virgil Shelby, Norman Tran, and Maudlin Vo.
