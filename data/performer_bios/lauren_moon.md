**Lauren Moon** (she/they) is a music lover living in Los Angeles. You can catch her spinning her favorite femme-driven pop from here and abroad at community events and local radio stations. She is a regular DJ at Little Tokyo's Tuesday Night Cafe and broadcasts a monthly show on KCHUNG Radio 1630AM from a Chinatown pho restaurant. Outside of DJing, Lauren Moon is a writer and producer and has written for shows like Netflix's *Atypical*, *Dash & Lily,* and *Ransom Canyon*. She was at the very first Asian AF ten years ago!
