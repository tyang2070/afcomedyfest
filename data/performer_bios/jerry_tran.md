**Jerry Tran** is a Chicago-based comedian known for meticulously crafted material and a commanding, composed stage presence. A veteran of the national circuit, Jerry has performed at SF Sketchfest, the Milwaukee Comedy Festival, and the NBC Second City Break Out Comedy Festival. In addition to his festival appearances, Jerry produces and hosts a recurring showcase at First Sip Cafe, a staple of Chicago’s historic Argyle neighborhood. His sharp, observational style earned him a spot on Cold Tea Collective’s list of “Eight North American Asian Comedians to Watch,” and he recently taped a featured episode for OnlyFans’ LMAOF comedy special.
