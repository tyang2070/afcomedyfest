Formed in 2016, **Voltron** is Asian AF's resident improv team, bringing together legendary Asian American improvisers from the UCB Theatre. The team hosts Voltron and Friends, a monthly improv comedy show, featuring a rotating lineup of emerging and established Asian American improvisers. 

Cast: **Lilan Bowden, Sarah Claspell, Kim Cooper, Eugene Cordero, Zac Oyama, Allyn Pintal, Dhruv Uday Singh, Kathy Yamamoto**
