**Embarrassed by Night Presents: The Viet-Down**

**Embarrassed by Night**, the premiere all-Vietnamese standup & variety showcase created and hosted by **Fred Le** and **Andy Van**, makes its long-awaited collaboration with Asian AF with a special edition of the show featuring a few of the best half-Vietnamese comedians in the world, each vying to answer the rarely-asked question : Which ethnicity is the *most* Vietnamese? This is THE VIET-DOWN!

In this one-off show, hilarious headliners **Sammy Cantu** (half-Mexican), **Lillian Sam** (half-Chinese), and **Joseph Nguyen** (half-Jewish) will each take the stage with a standup set touting their extra ethnicity to share with the audience and our hosts about why they are the 'most' Vietnamese. At the end of the hour, the audience and judging panel will vote to determine who gets to own the title of 'VIETNAMESIEST'! You do not want to miss this experience, because we're probably only gonna ever do it once, and what better of a place to try this shit out than at the inaugural festival put on by the homies ASIAN AF?

Cast: **Sammy Cantu, Fred Le, Joseph Nguyen, Lillian Sam, Andy Van**
