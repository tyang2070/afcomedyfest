**Dylan Adler** is a comedian, actor, writer, and musician based in NYC and LA. He was a writer on *The Late Late Show with James Corden* where he also performed [Stand Up](https://youtu.be/q9VbNnxff3Q) and was a [Sports Correspondent](https://youtu.be/OGquXbVPc1I). He recently performed stand up on [Comedy Central](https://youtu.be/c3XvApJvFvM?si=RKy_KfiML-Orisdm) and was named a [Comic You Should and Will Know](https://www.vulture.com/article/dylan-adler-comedians-you-should-know-2022.html) by Vulture Magazine. He has also performed in the New York Comedy Festival as a [Comic To Watch](https://youtu.be/T8rSjUk7UpY).
