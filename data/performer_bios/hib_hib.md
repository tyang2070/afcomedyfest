**Hib Hib** (pronounced "Hee - Hee") is the first and only Hmong American Sketch comedy show in California. It is put on in the city of Fresno, Ca, during the Fresno Hmong New Years and features Hmong American performers and writers from all over the US. Since 2022, Hib Hib continues to produce sold out shows in Fresno and have become advocates for spreading Sketch/Improv comedy to the Southeast Asian communities of the central valley.
