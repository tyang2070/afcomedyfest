**Brent Weinbach** is a recipient of the Andy Kaufman Award, which recognizes innovation in stand-up comedy.  He is also responsible for various internet video sensations such as “Gangster Party Line,” “Mind Jack,” “Man Gives Birth,” and “Ultimate Drumming Technique,” as well as the cult web series, *Pound House*.  Brent has appeared on lots of TV shows and also toured with the Comedians of Comedy.  His stand-up special "Popular Culture" was featured in New York Times’ Best Comedy of 2025 list.
