**A++** is an Asian American improv comedy team based in Baltimore. The seven members first connected during classes at the Baltimore Improv Group and formed the team in 2019. Because the cast shares a similar upbringing, they have an unspoken understanding that lets them turn real stories and cultural experiences into comedy.

Cast: **Grant Chang, Sam Brunner, Keith Estrella, Kim Le, Heather Carlson, Jacob Joseph, Brian Young**
