**Will Pepper** is a Vietnamese American comedian, actor, and writer based in Los Angeles.

He has headlined the New York Comedy Festival, performed at the Melbourne International Comedy Festival, and been featured on Netflix Is A Joke Radio. A recurring performer on the *Uncle Roger Show* — with episodes surpassing 10 million views — Will has toured internationally opening for Jason Cheny and Jimmy O. Yang. A two-time winner of Utah's Comedy Rumble, he has performed at The Comedy Store, Hollywood Improv, and the Laugh Factory.

On screen, Will has a cooking show with his dad that has garnered millions of views and has appeared in national commercials for Apple, Best Buy, and Amazon. He also starred in the short film *Happy Thoughts*, currently making its festival run.
