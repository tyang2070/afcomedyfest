A fan favorite on NPR's hit *Wait Wait Don't Tell Me*, **Helen Hong** also hosts NPR's celebrity trivia gameshow *Go Fact Yourself*. Helen's acting credits include roles in A24's *If I Had Legs I'd Kick You*, *Never Have I Ever*, *Silicon Valley,* and 5 seasons of *The Thundermans* on Nickelodeon. A breast cancer survivor, Asian-American activist, and frequent dog foster parent, Helen uses her standup comedy skills to raise funds and awareness for her favorite causes.
