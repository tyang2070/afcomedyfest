As an actor, **Woody Fu** (he/him) has appeared on *Pluribus*, *The Other Two*, *No Good Deed*, and the upcoming CBS series *Einstein*. 

As a creator, you may have seen him as a New Face of Comedy at the Just For Laughs festival, or in Edinburgh Fringe touring his show Woody Fu: One Man John Wick. 

He has written for the Onion, and his digital shorts have been featured on Vulture and at the Slamdance Film Festival.
