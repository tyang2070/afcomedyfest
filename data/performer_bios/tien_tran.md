**Tien Tran** is a Vietnamese-American comedian, actor, and writer. She starred as “Ellen” in *How I Met Your Father* (Hulu) and “Lucy” in *Mr. Throwback* (Peacock). Recently named Time Out’s Comics to Watch 2025, her stand-up has been featured on Comedy Central's *Taking the Stage* and at the Just for Laughs Festival in Montreal as part of the 2019 New Faces of Comedy showcase. She’s written for *Work in Progress* (Showtime) and the Emmy-nominated *Book of Queer* (Discovery+). You can find her on IG @hanktina.
