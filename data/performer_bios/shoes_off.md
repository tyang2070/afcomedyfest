We are **Shoes Off**, an all-Asian improv team and community based out of NYC\! We produce our own bimonthly show at the Magnet Theater for Asian improvisors, and we regularly perform at festivals, including Fun And Dumb (New York), Third Coast Improv Fest (Nashville), and Hartford Improv Fest. Our form is the Lotus, a half-Onion opening into a montage. Our sets are generally fast-paced with quick transitions, and scenes are focused on characters and relationships a la Magnet Theater where we are based. Several of our members are on Megawatt, Magnet's house teams.
