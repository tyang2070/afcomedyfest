**Leslie Liao** is a stand-up comedian, actress, and writer. Audiences around the world have been drawn to her relatable, introspective, and matter-of-fact style of humor, which is often anchored in her experiences as a single Asian-American woman living in Los Angeles.

Leslie was recently selected as one of Variety's Top 10 Comics to Watch at Montreal's prestigious Just for Laughs Comedy Festival, having first been named a New Face by the festival in 2023\.

She can be seen in the multi-comic stand-up series *Verified Stand-Up* on Netflix, and has performed twice on *The Tonight Show Starring Jimmy Fallon*. In addition to touring globally and headlining to sold-out crowds, Leslie has opened for some of the biggest names in comedy, including Fortune Feimster and Ronny Chieng.

She is a regular presence in the Los Angeles comedy scene, gracing the stages of iconic venues like The Comedy Store, Hollywood Improv, Laugh Factory, Dynasty Typewriter, and many more.
