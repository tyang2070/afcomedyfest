**Teruko Nakajima** is an actor, dancer, singer, writer, sculptor, and your biggest cheerleader on earth—she’ll cheer the heck you up until you say ENOUGH:) She’s also a proud single mother to her adopted baby doggie, Titi (“Tiny Teruko”), who is a psychiatric service animal (supporting everyone, whether you asked or not). Teruko, Titi, and Mr. John Flynn created Teruko’s raw autobiographical solo show, *MADE IN AMERICA*. It’s won 7 awards, became an Off-Broadway show, and completely went to Titi’s head. Teruko and Titi are super honored Upright Citizens Brigade performers.
