**Lillian Sam** is a Vietnamese/Chinese American bred comedian. Lillian "grew up" (comedy wise) in San Francisco where she started stand up comedy. Now in LA, Lillian has branched into different sectors of comedy including improv and sketch writing. Using personal anecdotes, observations, and public shaming, Lillian's humor has garnered 3 comedy competition wins selected from peers and judges alike. She has no friends so you know these wins are for real. Lillian's mantra is "send it".
